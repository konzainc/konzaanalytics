KONZA Internal Operational Alerts – Opt-In

Program Summary

KONZA uses SMS to deliver internal operational alerts to authorized KONZA employees and staff. These alerts may include notifications about infrastructure events, production failures, performance degradation, and other system issues that may affect customer-facing services.

By opting in, employees consent to receive internal operational SMS alerts from KONZA. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For assistance, reply HELP or email biainfocrmtool@konza.org.

Message frequency: Event-driven; varies based on incident volume.

Message & data rates may apply.

Opt-Out Instructions

Reply STOP to stop receiving SMS alerts. Supported opt-out keywords include: STOP, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, and QUIT. You will receive a confirmation message and no further alerts unless you re-opt in. If supported by your carrier, you may resume messages by replying START.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance or email biainfocrmtool@konza.org.
