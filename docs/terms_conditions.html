KONZA Internal Operational Alerts – Terms & Conditions

These Terms & Conditions ("Terms") govern participation in the KONZA Internal Operational Alerts SMS program (the "Program"). By enrolling, you agree to receive event‑driven, internal operational text messages from KONZA to the mobile number you provided and to abide by these Terms.

Program Scope

The Program delivers system and infrastructure notifications to authorized KONZA employees and staff. Messages may include alerts about production failures, performance degradation, maintenance windows, and other operational events that could affect customer‑facing services. This Program is not intended for external/consumer recipients.

Enrollment & Consent

Participation requires enrollment via KONZA’s secure opt‑in workflow. By opting in, you authorize KONZA to send SMS messages related to internal operations to your mobile number. Message frequency is event‑driven and may vary.

Opt‑Out & Help

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. Recognized opt‑out keywords include STOP, END, CANCEL, UNSUBSCRIBE, and QUIT. A confirmation message will be sent and no further alerts will be delivered unless you re‑opt in.

For assistance, reply HELP or email mailto:biainfocrmtool@konza.orgbiainfocrmtool@konza.org.

Employee Responsibilities

  • Maintain current mobile contact information in KONZA systems.
  • Safeguard operational information received via SMS in accordance with KONZA policies.
  • Report misdirected or inappropriate alerts to your supervisor or IT Service Desk.

Acceptable Use

The Program is for operational notifications only. Use of the Program for marketing, solicitation, or non‑operational purposes is prohibited. KONZA may suspend or terminate participation for misuse or violation of these Terms or applicable policies.
